# Chargeback360

Chargeback360 dispute and chargeback management solutions for merchants and payment processors fully integrates to Next Commerce.

{% hint style="info" %}
Chargeback360 is an App, installable via the Apps menu in your store.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

The Chargeback360 App is integrated to seamlessly connect your account and disputes management workflows with Next Commerce:

* Automated processing of Prevention Alerts, Order Matching, and providing evidence for dispute responses.
* Automated processing of Chargebacks, Order Matching, and adding cards to **Block Lists**

### How to Connect Chargeback360 to Your Store

Simply navigate to **Apps > Chargeback360** and choose to Install App.

The Chargeback360 App automatically creates the credentials it requires in your store to be able to access transactions and manage disputes.
