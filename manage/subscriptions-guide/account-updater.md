# Account Updater

Recover revenue and avoid involuntary churn from your subscription customers by enrolling in Account Updater.

When enabled, Account Updater automatically updates expiring bankcards for customers with active subscriptions to prevent failed subscription renewals. &#x20;

Successful account updates leading to recovered revenue are shown on the [**Subscriptions Recoveries**](../../analytics/subscription-performance.md#subscriptions-recoveries-report) report.

### Enable Account Updater

From **Settings > Payments**, choose **Enable Account Updater** on the **Settings** card.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Note **Account Updater** is a paid service.  When enabled it will incur additional fees on your NEXT account.  Contact your Next Commerce account manager for more details.&#x20;
{% endhint %}
