# Subscription Settings

## **Subscriptions Settings**

From the **Settings > Subscriptions** area, configure settings that will control your store's handling of subscription events.

{% hint style="info" %}
Subscriptions are visible to customers in their storefront **Customer Accounts**.  Review the Customer Accounts [**subscription settings**](../../build-a-store/storefront/customer-accounts.md#subscriptions) to ensure your subscriber experience and self-service options are presented as intended.
{% endhint %}

### Cancel Reasons

Subscription cancellation reasons are standardized platform-wide. See [**Cancellation Paths**](cancellation-paths.md) for the full list.

### Failed Payments Handling

Configure settings related to the handling of failed subscription renewal payments. See [**Subscription Cancellation Paths**](cancellation-paths.md) and [**Subscription Renewal Decline Salvage**](decline-salvage.md) for more detail on each feature set.

### Notifications

Configure **Upcoming Renewal Notifications** to notify your customers via email of upcoming recurring billing events, either for all billing events, or only prior to the first recurring charge in a subscription. &#x20;

From this card you can also choose whether to trigger the sending of the Order Confirmation [**notification**](../../start-here/get-started/support-and-notifications.md#notifications) on recurring orders.

The available Subscription Notifications are as follows.  It's recommended that all of these should be set to Active in your store's [**Notifications Settings**](../../start-here/get-started/support-and-notifications.md#notifications).

* **Subscription Renewal Notification**
* **Subscription Canceled Notification**
* **Subscription Update Payment Notification** - when enabled, a customer will be notified when their subscription fails to renew, with a one-click link to allow them to enter a new payment method.&#x20;
