# Advanced Settings

## Advanced **Fulfillment Settings**

In **Settings > Fulfillment** you can configure additional settings to control when orders are sent to fulfillment locations for shipping.

Toggle whether orders should automatically be sent to your integrated Fulfillment Service App, for example [**ShipStation**](../../apps/shipstation.md), or handled manually.

You may also configure an optional **Automatic fulfillment processing delay** to allow orders to remain in **Confirmed** status for a set number of hours, before being transitioned to **Processing** status and sent to fulfillment. &#x20;

This setting is useful if your customers often wish to modify or cancel their orders, or update their address details, soon after placing them.  Once orders are sent to fulfillment, and transition to **Processing** status, they may no longer be able to be modified via the Dashboard (certain exceptions apply).

Check out the Orders Guide for more information on order [**Status Changes**](../../manage/orders/#status-changes).
