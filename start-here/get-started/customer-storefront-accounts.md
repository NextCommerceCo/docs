# Customer Storefront Accounts

## Customer Accounts Settings

Navigate to **Settings > Customer Accounts** to customize the look and feel of your storefront’s [**Customer Accounts**](../../build-a-store/storefront/customer-accounts.md) section.

{% hint style="info" %}
Prefer to further customize your Customer Accounts functionality, and what is available to your customers?  Ask your NEXT account representative for customization options.
{% endhint %}

### Order Management

Select the **Allow Self-serve Order Cancellation** checkbox to expose a **Cancel** button on the Orders view of the storefront Customer Account. &#x20;

Offering this **Cancel** option allows customers to cancel their own orders before they are sent for fulfillment.  Disable this setting if you want to prevent customers from cancelling their own orders.

{% hint style="danger" %}
**IMPORTANT NOTE -** Customers canceling Unfulfilled orders in Paid status will have their payments automatically refunded. &#x20;
{% endhint %}

### Subscription Management

Select the **Allow customers to manage their subscription renewal cycles** checkbox to expose a **Change Next Renewal Date** button on the Subscriptions view of the storefront Customer Account, and a **Change Schedule** button if the subscription supports more than one [**billing interval** ](../../build-a-store/catalogue/add-products.md#purchase-options)option.

&#x20;Disable this setting if you want to prevent customers from modifying their own subscriptions.

### Support Management

Select the **Allow customers to create support tickets** checkbox to selectively show the [**Support**](../../build-a-store/storefront/customer-accounts.md#support) view in the storefront Customer Account. &#x20;

Disable this setting if you want to prevent customers from having the ability to create Support Tickets from inside their accounts.
