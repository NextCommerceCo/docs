# Upsells & SEO Settings

From the [**Product Details View**](add-products.md#product-details-view) you can add optional content and settings to customize the functionality of your storefront product page.

### Recommended Products

You may optionally choose to associate **Recommended Products** to your product, which will appear on the storefront product page for your customers to add to their orders.  Assign a Priority to these products to control which upsell items will display first.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Theme Developers** **-** to learn about custom storefront product pages, and how to create and customize theme templates, check out the Next Commerce [**developer docs**](https://developers.29next.com/docs/themes/guides/custom-product-templates/)**.**
{% endhint %}

### SEO Settings

Customize the following content for SEO purposes.  If your store supports [**multiple languages**](../../start-here/get-started/add-locations-and-languages.md#additional-languages) you may input translated content using the language tabs on the content fields.

* Meta title
* Meta description
