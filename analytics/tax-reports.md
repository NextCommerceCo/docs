# Tax Reports

Tax reports break down total orders, line items, revenue, refunds, net revenue, and total tax collected, organized by tax rate and tax class.

#### Tax by Tax Rate

View aggregated tax collection metrics by [**Tax Rate**](../build-a-store/catalogue/tax-settings.md#tax-rates) and [**Tax Class**](../build-a-store/catalogue/tax-settings.md#tax-classes) for orders created during the date range selected.  Export the results by selecting the **Download CSV** button.&#x20;
