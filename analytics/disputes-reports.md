# Disputes Reports

Dispute reports allow you to view and analyze [**payment disputes**](../features/payments/disputes-guide.md), aggregated by a variety of dimensions. Choose **Filters** to narrow down the results by various metrics, and export the results by choosing **Download CSV**.

Disputes Reports display the below aggregated data points for Disputes marked within the specified date range:

* Disputes
* Ratio (%) - the ratio of Disputes to debit Transactions
* Total Value ($)
* Avg. Value($)
* Avg. Time to Dispute (Days)\*

{% hint style="info" %}
Disputes data is available for transactions that have been marked as Disputed, either [**manually**](../features/payments/disputes-guide.md#disputes-list-view) or automatically by a third party App.\
\
Developers can learn more about how to build automation around the management of Disputes with a [**Disputes Service App**](https://developers.29next.com/docs/apps/guides/dispute-service/).
{% endhint %}

#### **Disputes Overview**

View a breakdown of individual transactions with associated payment disputes across your entire store for the date range specified.

#### **Disputes by Type**

View a breakdown of payment disputes by [**dispute type**](../features/payments/disputes-guide.md#disputes). Click on the disputes totals to drill down and view a list of individual disputes, filtered by type.

#### **Disputes by Gateway**

View a breakdown of disputes by payment gateway. Click on the disputes totals to drill down and view a list of individual disputes, filtered by the payment gateway on which the underlying transaction was processed.

#### **Disputes by Card BIN**

View a breakdown of disputes by Card BIN (bank identification number).  Click on the disputes totals to drill down and view a list of disputes, filtered by the cardholder's BIN.
