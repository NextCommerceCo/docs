# Transactions Reports

Transaction reports allow you to view and analyze payment transactions, aggregated by a variety of dimensions.  Choose **Filters** to narrow down the results by other metrics, and export the results by choosing **Download CSV**.

{% hint style="info" %}
Note: **Transactions** differ from **Orders**. Transactions are unique payment events, while Orders result only from a completed purchase by a Customer, and may comprise multiple payment transactions.
{% endhint %}

Drill-down on the report results to view a list of the unique transactions, where you can further filter the results to isolate specific transactions by a variety of metrics.

Transactions Reports display the below aggregated data points for transactions made within the specified date range.  For example, any sales and refund transactions that were processed during the date range specified would be reflected in the report totals.

* Transactions    &#x20;
* Success    &#x20;
* Failed
* Disputes Count
* Avg. Value  &#x20;
* Success Rate %
* Authorized
* Void
* Debit (Charged)
* Refunds    &#x20;
* Net Revenue&#x20;

Hover over the **Refunds** and **Net Revenue** totals to view the values in percentages (%).

#### Transactions by Payment Method

View a breakdown of transactions by app, for example Bankcard v. Paypal.  Click on the Transactions totals to drill down and view a list of individual transactions.

#### Transactions by UTM

View a breakdown of transactions by UTM\_Campaign, Source, Medium, Term, and Content values, based on the `utm` values that can optionally be passed into order [**source attribution**](../features/offers/marketing-attribution.md) fields.  Click on the Transactions totals to drill down and view a list of individual transactions.

#### Transactions by Affiliate

View a breakdown of transactions by Affiliate values from order [**source attribution**](../features/offers/marketing-attribution.md).  Click on the Transactions totals to drill down and view a list of individual transactions, or click on the Affiliate name to drill-down to display Sub-Affiliate, Sub-Affiliate1, 2, data.

#### Transactions by Funnel

View a breakdown of transactions by [**Funnel ID**](../build-a-store/storefront/funnels.md). Click on the Transactions totals to drill down and view a list of individual transactions for each given source Funnel.

#### Transactions by Response Code

View a breakdown of transactions by response codes from the payment gateway. Click on the Transactions totals to drill down and view a list of individual transactions for each given response code.

#### Transactions by Card BIN

View a breakdown of transactions by the customer card issuing BIN (bank identification number), which is equivalent to a bankcard's first 6 digits. Click on the Transactions totals to drill down and view a list of individual transactions for each given Card BIN.  This report helps to identify the prevalence and billing performance of various card issuers.&#x20;

#### Transactions by **Gateway**

View a breakdown of transactions by all available payment gateways. Click on the Transactions totals to drill down and view a list of individual transactions for each given payment gateway.  This report helps to identify the and compare the performance of the different gateways that are set up to process payments in your store.
