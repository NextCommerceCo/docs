## New Features

- Tax settings have been overhauled to bring more clarity and flexibility to assign Tax Services to Tax Regions allow for multiple tax services at the same time.
- Stripe Link is now supported as an express checkout method for a faster, lower friction payment experience.

## Improvements

- [Campaigns App](https://docs.nextcommerce.com/docs/apps/campaigns-app) now always shows for all users in the dashboard for all users who have access to the app.
- Storefront Checkout now supports South Korea, Japan, China, and Israel and their local respective currencies.
- We've improved our background CSV export process to be more robust and less likely to
- German translations on the Checkout flow have been improved to match customer expectations in German speaking markets.
- We've improved our logic and handling to always pass a customer phone number to shopify on the shipping address if one is possibly available.
- PayPal now supports adding custom soft descriptor statement descriptor through the dashboard settings, Campaign settings, and also per order using the `statement_descriptor` [Admin API](https://developers.nextcommerce.com/docs/admin-api).

## Bug Fixes

- We fixed an issue with `product.updated` webhooks not being able to send retries if the product images were deleted.
- We fixed an issue with subscription next renewal date was not set properly after the subscription recovery flow succeeded.
- We fixed an issue with campaign integration modal not showing after a second click.
