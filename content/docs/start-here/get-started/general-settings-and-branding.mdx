## General Settings

From the **Settings** menu, start on the **General** tab.

Input the “Store Name” that will appear in your web store’s header and Dashboard title, and add an optional “Store Tagline” to insert into your HTML metadata.

Configure the **Timezone** that will be used for the display of all events and **Reports**.

<Callout type="info">

It’s useful to set the store timezone to match any external conversion tracking software you may use, to avoid discrepancies between order data and tracking data.

</Callout>

If your store theme supports customer [**Reviews**](/docs/build-a-store/storefront/reviews) of your products, you may wish to check the **Moderate Reviews** checkbox. This will require that any customer review is approved by a store administrator before being published to the live storefront.  Access pending reviews via **Storefront > Reviews.**

### **Contact Information**

Optionally add public store contact information that is displayed on your storefront theme, and on customer [**notification emails**](/docs/start-here/get-started/support-and-notifications#notifications) and invoices.

### **SEO & Social Settings**

From General Settings you can optionally configure SEO settings to customize how your store will appear in search engine results.

* Meta Title
* Meta Description
* Social Preview Image

## **Store Branding**

Next, from the **Settings** menu, select the **Branding** tab.

Set your store logo and favicon, along with your brand's primary and secondary theme colors from this tab.

The store logo is displayed to customers in transactional [**notification** **emails**](/docs/start-here/get-started/support-and-notifications#notifications), and on the storefront checkout.
