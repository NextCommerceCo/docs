Use this guide when you're setting up a NEXT store for the first time.

As a first step, login to your [**NEXT Account**](https://accounts.29next.com) to view the store(s) you have access to.

Click **Dashboard** to access the store's admin area.

<Callout type="info">

Shopify merchants connecting NEXT should start by installing [**Shop Sync**](/docs/apps/shop-sync).

</Callout>

### ⚡️ START HERE

**Preparing to Go Live?**

A store should have a primary domain, payment processing, fulfillment, shipping, notifications, and at least one sellable product configured before accepting live orders. Stores using apps such as Shop Sync, Campaigns App, or NEXT Payments may have additional setup requirements.

### Technical Settings

Once your basic settings are in place, you may choose to grant [**API access**](/docs/build-a-store/technical-settings#api-access) for your developers and third-party apps. You can also configure [**Webhooks**](/docs/build-a-store/technical-settings/configure-webhooks) or [**Redirects**](/docs/build-a-store/storefront/storefront-redirects) to enable custom functionality.

Check out the NEXT [**Developers Documentation**](https://developers.nextcommerce.com/) for more information on building apps and integrations.

<Cards>
  <Card title="Technical Settings" description="Set up API users, metadata, and other advanced features" href="/docs/build-a-store/technical-settings" />
</Cards>
### Conditional Settings

At this stage, configure [**Sales Tax**](/docs/build-a-store/catalogue/tax-settings), [**Subscription**](/docs/manage/subscriptions-guide/subscription-settings) settings, and custom [**Tags**](/docs/build-a-store/technical-settings/metadata-fields-and-tags#tags) if they apply to your launch. Otherwise, you can revisit these areas later.

### Apps

Apps and extensions can be enabled and configured at this stage. For example, if you plan to accept [**PayPal**](/docs/features/payments/paypal) in your checkout, you should enable the PayPal app and enter your account credentials before launch.

For extensions related to shipping and fulfillment, such as [**ShipStation**](/docs/apps/shipstation), remember to return to [**Fulfillment Settings > Locations**](/docs/start-here/get-started/fulfillment-settings#fulfillment-locations) after setup, and ensure that any locations are updated so the “Processing By” setting points orders to your preferred fulfillment app integration.

Once your core settings have been configured, you’re ready to add products to your store’s product catalogue.
