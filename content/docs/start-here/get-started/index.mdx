Use this reference guide when you’re setting up a NEXT store for the first time.

As a first step, login to your [**NEXT Account**](https://accounts.29next.com) to view the store(s) you have access to.

Click **Dashboard** to access the store's admin area.

<Callout type="info">

Shopify merchants connecting NEXT should start by installing [**Shop Sync**](/docs/apps/shop-sync)

</Callout>

### ⚡️ START HERE

### Essential Settings

Navigate to the **Settings** menu to begin configuring your store.

Follow our step by step Getting Started guides to add essential settings:

### Technical Settings

Once your basic settings are in place, you may choose to grant [**API access**](/docs/build-a-store/technical-settings#api-access) for your developers and third party Apps.   Also, configure [**Webhooks**](/docs/build-a-store/technical-settings/configure-webhooks) or [**Redirects**](/docs/build-a-store/storefront/storefront-redirects) to enable custom functionalities.

Check out the NEXT [**Developers Documentation**](https://developers.nextcommerce.com/) for more info on building Apps and integrations.

<Cards>
  <Card title="Technical Settings" description="Set up API Users, Metadata and other advanced features" href="/docs/build-a-store/technical-settings" />
</Cards>
### Optional Settings

At this stage you may wish to implement [**Sales Tax**](/docs/build-a-store/catalogue/tax-settings), [**Subscription**](/docs/manage/subscriptions-guide/subscription-settings) settings, and customize [**Tags**](/docs/build-a-store/technical-settings/metadata-fields-and-tags#tags) for segmenting events and data. Otherwise, you may revisit these areas at any time.

### Apps

Apps and extensions can be enabled and configured at this stage.  For example, if you plan to accept [**PayPal**](/docs/features/payments/paypal) in your checkout, you should enable the PayPal app and input your account credentials.

For extensions related to shipping and fulfillment, such as [**Shipstation**](/docs/apps/shipstation), remember to return to [**Fulfillment Settings > Locations**](/docs/start-here/get-started/fulfillment-settings#fulfillment-locations) after setup, and ensure that any Locations are updated so that the “Processing By” setting is configured to point orders to your preferred fulfillment App integration.

Once your core settings have been configured, you’re ready to add products to your store’s product catalogue.
