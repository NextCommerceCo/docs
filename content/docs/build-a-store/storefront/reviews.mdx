Product reviews let your customers share feedback on purchases, building trust and driving conversions on your storefront.  Manage reviews from the **Storefront > Reviews** menu.

## Reviews List

The reviews list displays all reviews with the following columns:

* **Title** — the review headline
* **Product** — the product the review is for (linked to the product page)
* **User** — the reviewer's name
* **Score** — rating out of 5.0
* **Votes** — helpfulness votes from other customers
* **Status** — moderation status (e.g. "Requires moderation", "Approved", "Rejected")
* **Date Created**

Use the **search bar** to find reviews by keyword, and **Filters** to narrow by date, status, product, score, or other criteria.

### Bulk Actions

Select one or more reviews using the checkboxes, then use the **Actions** dropdown to approve or reject them in bulk.  This is the fastest way to moderate a queue of incoming reviews.

## Review Detail

Click on a review title to open the detail view.  The left panel shows editable fields:

* **Title** — the review headline (editable for moderation)
* **Body** — the full review text
* **Score** — rating dropdown (adjustable)
* **Status** — set to Approved, Requires moderation, or Rejected

The right **Review Detail** panel shows read-only information: **Customer** name, **Product** link, **Date**, and current **Status**.

## Moderation

By default, new reviews are submitted with a "Requires moderation" status and will not appear on the storefront until approved.

To configure moderation behavior, go to **Settings > General Settings** and look for the **Moderate Reviews** option.  When enabled, all reviews must be approved by an administrator before they are published on the storefront.

<Callout type="info">

You can edit the title, body, and score of a review before approving it.  This is useful for correcting formatting issues or removing inappropriate content while preserving the customer's feedback.

</Callout>

## Who Can Leave Reviews

Reviews include built-in validation to prevent spam and ensure quality feedback:

**Allowed:**
* Logged-in registered users
* Non-logged-in customers who have placed at least one order (identified by email)

**Not allowed:**
* Users who do not exist in the system
* Leads who have never completed an order (e.g. abandoned checkout with email only)

This order-based validation ensures that only real customers can leave reviews, without requiring every reviewer to create an account.
