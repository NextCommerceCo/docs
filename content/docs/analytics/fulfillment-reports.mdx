---
title: "Fulfillment Reports"
description: "View aggregated metrics by fulfillment and delivery status"
---
Fulfillment reports allow you to view individual and aggregated fulfillments, filterable by a variety of dimensions. Choose **Filters** to narrow down the results by other metrics, and export the results by choosing **Download CSV**.

Drill-down on the report results to access a list of the unique orders that were fulfilled.

Fulfillment Reports display the below aggregated data points for orders placed within the chosen date range:

* Fulfillments
* Items
* Deliveries\*
* Failures\*
* Avg. Fulfillment Time (Days)
* Avg. Delivery Time (Days)\*

<Callout type="info">

\*Deliveries, Failures, and Avg. Delivery Time are available only in stores with the [**Delivery Tracking**](/docs/apps/delivery-tracking) App installed.

</Callout>

#### **Fulfillment Overview**

View a breakdown of individual orders fulfilled across your entire store for the date range specified.

#### **Fulfillment by Location**

View a breakdown of fulfillments by [**Fulfillment Location**](/docs/start-here/get-started/fulfillment-settings#fulfillment-locations). Click on the fulfillment totals to drill down and view a list of individual order fulfillments, filtered by location.

#### **Fulfillment by Destination**

View a breakdown of fulfillments by destination country. Click on the fulfillment totals to drill down and view a list of individual order fulfillments, filtered by destination.

#### **Fulfillment by Carrier**

View a breakdown of fulfillments by delivery carrier. Click on the fulfillment totals to drill down and view a list of individual order fulfillments, filtered by carrier.
