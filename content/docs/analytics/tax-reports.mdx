---
title: "Tax Reports"
description: "View summarized tax collection data across built-in and service-based tax regions"
---
Tax reports break down total orders, line items, revenue, refunds, net revenue, and total tax collected. They include tax collected through built-in NEXT Tax regions and regions assigned to TaxJar or Avalara AvaTax.

#### Tax by Tax Rate

For regions using built-in NEXT Tax, view aggregated tax collection metrics by [**Tax Rate**](/docs/build-a-store/catalogue/tax-settings#tax-rates) and [**Tax Class**](/docs/build-a-store/catalogue/tax-settings) for orders created during the selected date range. Export the results by selecting the **Download CSV** button.
