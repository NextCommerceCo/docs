---
title: "Tax Reports"
description: "View summarized tax collection data across all your product and sales channels"
---
Tax reports break down total orders, line items, revenue, refunds, net revenue, and total tax collected, organized by tax rate and tax class.

#### Tax by Tax Rate

View aggregated tax collection metrics by [**Tax Rate**](/docs/build-a-store/catalogue/tax-settings#tax-rates) and [**Tax Class**](/docs/build-a-store/catalogue/tax-settings) for orders created during the date range selected.  Export the results by selecting the **Download CSV** button.
