---
title: "Subscription Reports"
description: "Track subscriptions by lifecycle status, recurring and lifetime value, and MRR, with forecast, retention, and recovery reports."
---
Subscriptions reports display the total number of subscriptions by each lifecycle status, the average recurring billing value, and the average lifetime subscription value.  Choose **Filters** to narrow down the results by other metrics, and export the results by choosing **Download CSV**.

View aggregated subscription metrics by the below metrics, at each stage of the customer lifecycle:

* UTM Parameters
* Affiliate
* Funnel
* Product
* SKU
* Gateway

The below metrics are displayed aggregated as totals for the chosen date range.

* Subscriptions
* Active
* Retrying
* Past Due
* Canceled
* Average Recurring Value
* Average Lifetime Value
* MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)

For more insight into your subscription performance, cohort analysis, and cancel reasons, view the [**Subscriptions Dashboard.**](/docs/analytics/dashboards#subscriptions-dashboard)

### **Subscriptions Forecast Report**

The Subscription **Forecast** report displays a daily breakdown of expected subscription renewal orders, and the items contained therein, across a specific date-range.  Use this report to forecast your future subscription renewals and the effects on inventory demand.

For more details on setting up, managing, and evaluating Subscriptions, follow the link below.

### **Subscriptions Retention Report**

The Subscription **Retention** report displays a filterable cohort chart, showing the retention performance of subscribers in your store.  This cohort view is similar to the [**Subscriptions Dashboard**](/docs/analytics/dashboards#subscriptions-dashboard) view, but with the Retention report you can filter the chart by a variety of metrics such as marketing attribution sources, products, customer details, and [**metadata**](/docs/build-a-store/technical-settings/metadata-fields-and-tags#metadata) associated to the subscriptions.

### Subscriptions Recoveries Report

The Recoveries Report displays the aggregated amount of subscriptions and revenue recovered by Decline [**Retries**](/docs/manage/subscriptions-guide/decline-salvage#failed-payments-handling) using Smart Decline Salvage and [**Account Updater** ](/docs/manage/subscriptions-guide/account-updater)methods, for the date range specified.  This report is also shown visually on the [**Subscriptions Dashboard**](/docs/analytics/dashboards#subscriptions-dashboard).
