## **Subscriptions Settings**

From the **Settings > Subscriptions** area, configure settings that will control your store's handling of subscription events.

<Callout type="info">

Subscriptions are visible to customers in their storefront **Customer Accounts**.  Review the Customer Accounts [**subscription settings**](/docs/build-a-store/storefront/customer-accounts#subscriptions) to ensure your subscriber experience and self-service options are presented as intended.

</Callout>

### Cancel Reasons

Subscription cancellation reasons are standardized platform-wide. See [**Cancellation Paths**](/docs/manage/subscriptions-guide/cancellation-paths) for the full list.

When a customer or agent chooses **Other**, the cancellation flow presents a free-text field so the reason can be captured in the customer's own words. That text is stored with the cancellation and included in exports.

### Failed Payments Handling

Configure settings related to the handling of failed subscription renewal payments. See [**Subscription Cancellation Paths**](/docs/manage/subscriptions-guide/cancellation-paths) and [**Subscription Renewal Decline Salvage**](/docs/manage/subscriptions-guide/decline-salvage) for more detail on each feature set.

### Notifications

Configure **Upcoming Renewal Notifications** to notify your customers via email of upcoming recurring billing events, either for all billing events, or only prior to the first recurring charge in a subscription.

From this card you can also choose whether to trigger the sending of the Order Confirmation [**notification**](/docs/start-here/get-started/support-and-notifications#notifications) on recurring orders.

The available Subscription Notifications are as follows.  It's recommended that all of these should be set to Active in your store's [**Notifications Settings**](/docs/start-here/get-started/support-and-notifications#notifications).

* **Subscription Renewal Notification**
* **Subscription Canceled Notification**
* **Subscription Update Payment Notification** - when enabled, a customer will be notified when their subscription fails to renew, with a one-click link to allow them to enter a new payment method.
