## Subscription Pause

Subscriptions with a status of **Active** or **Retrying** can be paused. Pausing a subscription stops recurring billing only. All other subscription functionality continues to operate as normal.

When a subscription is paused, its status changes to **Paused** and any retry data (retry date and retry count) is cleared.

## Pause Types

When pausing a subscription, choose one of two pause types:

| Type | Behavior |
|---|---|
| **Indefinite** | Subscription remains paused until manually resumed. When you resume it, you choose the next renewal date. If not resumed within 6 months, the subscription is automatically canceled. |
| **Pause Until** | Subscription automatically resumes on the selected date. The date must be within 6 months of the pause start date. |

### Pause Until

Use **Pause Until** when you already know the day the subscription should come back.

The date you choose becomes the resume date, and the subscription automatically returns to **Active** on that date.

![Pause Subscription dialog with a custom resume date](</docs-assets/subscription-pause-until.png>)

### Pause Indefinitely

Use **Indefinitely** when you want to stop billing without picking a resume date yet.

The subscription stays paused until someone clicks **Resume**. If it is not resumed within 6 months, it is automatically canceled.

![Resume Subscription dialog with a future renewal date](</docs-assets/subscription-resume-date.png>)

## Resuming a Subscription

Resuming works differently depending on how the subscription was paused:

- If the subscription was paused with a resume date, it will automatically resume on that date.
- If the subscription was paused indefinitely, click **Resume** and choose the future **Next Renewal Date** that should reactivate the subscription.

## How to Pause a Subscription

Subscriptions can be paused from two places:

- **Dashboard** — From the [**Subscription Detail**](/docs/manage/subscriptions-guide/managing-subscriptions#subscription-details-view) view, select **Pause** from the available actions.
- **Customer Accounts** — Customers can pause their own subscriptions from their storefront [**Customer Accounts**](/docs/build-a-store/storefront/customer-accounts).
