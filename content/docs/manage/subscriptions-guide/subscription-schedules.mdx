## Subscription Schedules

Subscription schedules let store admins plan future changes to a subscription in advance. Changes are configured to apply automatically on a specified future date, allowing custom recurring billing events to be prepared and automated without manual intervention.

<Callout type="info">

Subscription schedules follow the same model as [Stripe Subscription Schedules](https://docs.stripe.com/billing/subscriptions/subscription-schedules).

</Callout>

## What Can Be Scheduled

From the **Subscription Detail** view, the following subscription properties can be configured to change at a future date:

* **Pricing** — Apply a new price to the subscription in advance (e.g. a promotional rate expiring on a set date)
* **Items** — Add or remove products from the subscription
* **Item quantities and prices** — Adjust the quantity or per-item price for any subscription line item
* **Shipping method and price** — Change the shipping method or override the shipping cost
* **Billing interval** — Change how frequently the subscription renews (e.g. switch from monthly to quarterly)
* **Change reason** — Attach a reason to the scheduled change (e.g. to note a partial refund adjustment)

## Upcoming Changes

Scheduled changes can be previewed on the **Upcoming Changes** card in the Subscription Detail view before they take effect. This gives a clear summary of what will change and when.

## Managing Schedules

Subscription schedules are managed from the **Subscription Detail** view. Navigate to **Subscriptions**, click **View** on a subscription, and use the schedule management options available in the detail view to create, edit, or remove scheduled changes.
