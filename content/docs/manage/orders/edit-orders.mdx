## Editing Orders

<Callout type="info">

**Editing Order Line Items**

After an order is placed you can edit the contents of the order for a limited amount of time.  While an order's items are in [**Unfulfilled status**](/docs/manage/orders/order-statuses#fulfillment-statuses), the contents of the order can be edited.

</Callout>

To add or remove items, or modify the quantities on an existing order, from the [**Order Details View** ](/docs/manage/orders/order-management#order-details-view)choose **Edit.**

Next, from the **Edit Order** View:

* **Add Products** - add additional product(s) to the order.
* **Adjust Quantity** - add or reduce quantities of the items already in the order.
* **Remove Items** - remove the current items from the order.
* **Reason for Edit** - specify a reason for the edit, which will be recorded on the order timeline.

To finish editing the order, click **Update Order.**

<Callout type="info">

Refunds are not automatically processed when performing order quantity downgrades, so they must be processed manually by using the [**Refund**](/docs/manage/orders/refund-items-and-orders) menu.\
\
When adding items or quantities to orders, you may be prompted to [**collect additional payment**](/docs/manage/orders/collect-payments-on-orders) from the customer. Merchants can collect the balance immediately or send the customer an invoice payment request.

</Callout>

### Edit Orders in Processing Status

Orders with items in a [**Processing**](/docs/manage/orders) fulfillment status can no longer be edited.

You may [**Request Cancellation**](/docs/manage/orders/order-management#request-fulfillment-cancellation) of the fulfillment items in Processing to stop fulfillment, (where supported by the fulfillment location).   If successfully canceled, you may then **Edit** the order, and then **Request Fulfillment** of the updated order.

<Callout type="info">

Customize the delay before **Unfulfilled** status order items are sent to your fulfillment location, and therefore transition to **Processing** status, by setting the fulfillment delay in [**Settings > Fulfillment**](/docs/start-here/get-started/fulfillment-settings)

</Callout>
