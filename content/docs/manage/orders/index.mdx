## Orders Overview

This article provides an overview of orders and the various concepts relating to orders that impact order management tasks.

## Orders List View

All orders can be found by navigating to the **Orders** menu.  Orders are listed from newest to oldest by default, and displayed along with their [**Orders Statuses**](/docs/manage/orders/order-statuses).

* Choose **Filters** to refine the Orders List according to a wide variety of filters, including [**Fulfillment Status**](/docs/features/fulfillment-guide/fulfillment-statuses).
* The **Search** bar field can find orders using the order number, customer name, phone, email, or any partial match or combination.
* Choose [**Create Order**](/docs/manage/orders/create-orders) to manually create a new order for a new or existing customer.

<Callout type="info">

Use [**Orders Reports**](/docs/analytics/orders-reports) to drill down from aggregated orders reporting data to filtered Orders List views.

</Callout>

From the Orders List view, click on the order number of any order, or on **View** to enter the [**Order Details**](/docs/manage/orders/order-management#order-details-view) view and perform order management tasks.

### Orders List Actions

Under the **Actions** menu the following options are available:

* **Export Orders** \
  Choose this option to export a CSV (comma separated value) file of the Orders List, with additional columns added including product, quantity, source attribution, payment methods, and more.  You can also create custom [**Metadata Fields**](/docs/build-a-store/technical-settings/metadata-fields-and-tags) with the option to enable them on exports.  <br />
* **Export Order Line Items**\
  Choose this option to export a CSV (comma separated value) file of the Orders List broken down by individual order [**Line Items**](/docs/manage/orders/order-management#order-items) in each order, listed on separate rows.  This export also includes extended data per line item such as product, quantity, source attribution, payment methods, and more.<br />
* **Delete Test Orders**\
  Choose this option to bulk remove [**Test Orders**](/docs/manage/orders/test-orders) data from the Orders List.  This is helpful to clean up your Orders List after performing tests on new campaigns, funnels, or order management workflows.
