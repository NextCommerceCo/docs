---
title: "Fraud Screening"
description: "Understand the automatic fraud screening applied to orders"
---

Next Commerce automatically screens orders for fraud risk as part of the platform's payment flow. No app installation or separate fraud-service credentials are required.

## Automatic Coverage

Fraud screening runs as a platform-managed service. Stores do not need to install the former MaxMind app or maintain a separate per-store integration.

This screening adds a risk signal to the order flow. It works alongside the fraud and dispute controls that merchants manage directly, including [**Block Lists**](/docs/features/payments/block-lists) and [**Dispute Handling**](/docs/features/payments/disputes-guide).

## Merchant Setup

There is no setup step for the core screening service. Continue to review payment disputes, keep store block-list rules current, and use your payment-account identifiers when enrolling in any additional dispute-alert services.

Contact your Next Commerce account representative if you need to confirm how fraud screening applies to a specific store or payment flow.
