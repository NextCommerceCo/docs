Next Commerce supports a range of alternative payment methods (APMs) beyond traditional bankcards, including Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL), bank transfers, and digital wallets. These methods are available on the storefront checkout, and many are also available for custom checkouts via the Admin API.

## Buy Now, Pay Later

BNPL methods let customers split purchases into installments at checkout, increasing conversion rates and average order values.

### Affirm

**Affirm** enables customers to pay over time with transparent, simple financing — no hidden fees or compounding interest. Affirm is available to merchants processing via **NEXT Payments**.

- Available in the **United States** and **Canada**
- Supports one-time purchases only — does not support recurring subscriptions or post-checkout upsells
- Customers choose from pay-in-4 (bi-weekly), monthly installments (3-36 months), or pay-in-full
- Merchant receives full payment upfront — Affirm handles collections and risk
- Minimum and maximum order amounts apply — consult Affirm's merchant documentation for current limits

To enable Affirm, first log in to your [**NEXT Payments account**](https://accounts.29next.com/) and apply to add Affirm as a payment method. Once approved, navigate to **Settings > Payments** in your store, select your NEXT Payments gateway, and enable **Affirm** from the **Alternative Payment Methods** card.

### Klarna

**Klarna** offers flexible payment options including Pay in 4, financing, and Pay Now (direct bank payment). Klarna is available to merchants processing via **NEXT Payments** or **Stripe**.

- Available in **19+ markets** across North America, Europe, and Oceania
- Supports Pay in 4 (interest-free installments), financing (6-36 months), and Pay Now
- Supports **recurring subscriptions** — Klarna tokenizes the payment method for future charges
- Supports **post-checkout upsells** — additional items can be charged to the same Klarna session
- Merchant receives full payment upfront — Klarna handles collections and risk

To enable Klarna via NEXT Payments, first log in to your [**NEXT Payments account**](https://accounts.29next.com/) and apply to add Klarna as a payment method. Once approved, navigate to **Settings > Payments** in your store, select your gateway, and enable **Klarna** from the **Alternative Payment Methods** card.

<Callout type="info">

Klarna availability and payment options vary by market. Consult [**Klarna's country availability**](https://www.klarna.com/international/) for details on which options are available in each region.

</Callout>

## Digital Wallets

### TWINT

**TWINT** is a Swiss mobile wallet that lets customers pay from their TWINT app at checkout. TWINT is available exclusively to merchants processing via **NEXT Payments**.

- Available for eligible merchants selling to customers in **Switzerland**
- Works as a redirect-based wallet payment during checkout
- Supports one-time purchases and recurring purchases
- Supports one-click upsells after checkout
- Merchant receives payment through the connected NEXT Payments account

To enable TWINT, first log in to your [**NEXT Payments account**](https://accounts.29next.com/) and apply to add TWINT as a payment method. Once approved, navigate to **Settings > Payments** in your store, select your NEXT Payments gateway, and enable **TWINT** from the **Alternative Payment Methods** card.

<Callout type="info">

TWINT is a **NEXT Payments exclusive** payment method. It is not available through Stripe gateways.

</Callout>

### Stripe Link

**Stripe Link** saves and autofills customer payment and shipping details across Stripe-powered checkouts, reducing friction and accelerating checkout.

- Available to merchants using **Stripe** as their gateway
- Customers save payment details once, then check out with one click on any Link-enabled site
- Supports card payments saved to the customer's Link account
- Available in all Stripe-supported markets

To enable Link, navigate to **Settings > Payments**, select your Stripe gateway, and enable **Link** from the **Alternative Payment Methods** card.

For [**Apple Pay**](/docs/features/payments/apple-pay) and [**Google Pay**](/docs/features/payments/google-pay), see their dedicated setup guides.

## European Bank Payments

The following bank payment methods are available for merchants using **Stripe** as their gateway. These are especially useful for merchants selling in European markets.

| Method | Markets | Type |
|--------|---------|------|
| **iDEAL** | Netherlands | Bank redirect |
| **Bancontact** | Belgium | Bank redirect |
| **Sofort** | Germany, Austria, Belgium, Netherlands, Spain | Bank redirect |
| **Giropay** | Germany | Bank redirect |
| **SEPA Direct Debit** | Eurozone (36 countries) | Bank debit |

To enable any of these methods, navigate to **Settings > Payments**, select your Stripe gateway, and enable the method from the **Alternative Payment Methods** card.

<Callout type="info">

These methods may be available in specific regions only. Consult Stripe's documentation for current availability and requirements per market.

</Callout>

For implementation details, start with the [**External Checkout Flow**](https://developers.nextcommerce.com/docs/admin-api/guides/external-checkout), then use the payment-method guides where available:
