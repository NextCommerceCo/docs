Apple Pay is supported as a payment method on the Next Commerce Storefront Checkout and over the [**Admin API**](https://developers.nextcommerce.com/docs/admin-api/guides/apple-pay).  Apple Pay allows customers using iOS, iPad, or Mac devices to securely and conveniently complete payments with a single tap.

Apple Pay is supported on the following gateways:

* NEXT Payments
* Adyen
* Airwallex\*\*
* Authorize.Net\*\*
* Braintree
* Checkout.com
* NMI\*\*
* Paysafe\*\*
* Stripe Payment Intents
* Test Gateway

<Callout type="warning">

\*\* Recurring transactions may not be supported on certain gateways.  Please contact support to confirm suport for Apple Pay recurring billing.

</Callout>

## Add Apple Pay to a Gateway

When you've added a supported payment gateway, enabling Apple Pay is simple:

* From the **Settings > Payments** view, choose a gateway ID
* On the Gateway Detail view, under **Apple Pay,** click **Enable**

<Callout type="info">

Apple Pay registers your store's [**primary domain**](/docs/start-here/get-started/link-domains#domains) for authenticating payments.

</Callout>
